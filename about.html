VELNOVA was born from a simple frustration — premium, well-fitting clothing that actually suits the Nigerian body and climate was hard to find without paying international prices. We changed that.

We design and source premium menswear and womenswear built for real life in Nigeria — the heat, the hustle, the occasions. Every piece is selected with intention and held to an uncompromising quality standard.

From boardrooms in Lagos to weekends in Abuja, VELNOVA is the wardrobe you reach for.
